Who am I and What I do

SRE

As a SRE, I will be responsible for ensuring the reliability, availability, and performance of software systems through a combination of automation, monitoring, incident response, and proactive engineering. Automate as much as possible of the operational tasks. By writing code and creating tools to manage and monitor systems, it's possible to automate operations such as provisioning, scalability, and error recovery. Automation aids in the reduction of human error and the improvement of system consistency. Set up real-time monitoring and warning systems to spot abnormalities and concerns. Develop dashboards and alerts to monitor system health and performance, allowing those to take proactive measures. Analyze system performance and aim to improve it. Identifying and resolving bottlenecks, enhancing code performance, and fine-tuning setups are all examples of this. Disaster recovery solutions are being used to protect company continuity in the event of data center failures or other catastrophic events.

DevOps

As a DevOps Engineer, my responsibilities will include automating processes, developing CI/CD pipelines, and managing infrastructure as code to bridge the gap between software development and IT operations. I will be in responsible for ensuring that software is delivered in an efficient, dependable, and secure manner, as well as monitoring and optimizing system performance. My responsibilities include collaboration, automation, and a focus on both development and operations, with the ultimate goal of accelerating software development cycles and improving system stability.
